Welcome to Alzatona

The first Arabic summarizer that allows you to choose between abstractive and extractive types of summarization using modern AI models.

What is abstractive summarization?

It could be defined as the summarization of text by teaching the model to process the input article and generate a summary that may contain new words, sentences, or phrases that were not previously present in the input article.

What is extractive summarization?

It could be defined as the summarization of text by teaching the model to separate the input reference article into sentences, to rank the sentences by their quality as representative of the input article, then choosing and concatenating the top sentences to form a coherent summary.
